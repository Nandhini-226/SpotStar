Raabta

Release Date: 9 June 2017

Director: Dinesh Vijan

he film tells the story of Shiv (Sushant Singh Rajput), a man who falls in love with Saira (Kriti Sanon), but their relationship seems to be influenced by mysterious forces. The story takes a supernatural turn as the couple discovers they were connected in a past life, and their love story spans multiple lifetimes. Raabta explores themes of destiny, reincarnation, and eternal love, with a blend of romance and fantasy. Despite the intriguing concept and the chemistry between the leads, the film received mixed reviews for its narrative and pacing. However, the soundtrack, including the hit song "Raabta," was well-received and became popular.
