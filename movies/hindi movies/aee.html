Ae Dil Hai Mushkil

Release Date: oct 28 2016

Director: Karan Johar

Ae Dil Hai Mushkil" (2016) is a Bollywood romantic drama directed by Karan Johar. It stars Ranbir Kapoor, Anushka Sharma, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan, and Fawad Khan. The story centers on Ayan (Ranbir Kapoor) and his unrequited love for Alizeh (Anushka Sharma), who only sees him as a friend. When Ayan meets Saba (Aishwarya Rai), a poet, he begins to rediscover himself. The film's powerful soundtrack, with hits like "Channa Mereya" and "Ae Dil Hai Mushkil", highlights the journey of love, friendship, and heartbreak.
