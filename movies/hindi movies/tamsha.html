Tamasha

Release Date: 27 November 2015

Director: Imtiaz Ali

The film explores the journey of Ved (Ranbir Kapoor), a man who is disconnected from his true self and is trapped in a monotonous life. He meets Tara (Deepika Padukone) during a trip to Corsica, where they decide to live without revealing their true identities, creating a fantasy world. As the story unfolds, the two realize the complexities of their personalities and the need to embrace their true selves, despite the societal pressures. Tamasha explores themes of self-discovery, societal expectations, and the conflict between personal desires and responsibilities.
