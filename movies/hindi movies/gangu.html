Gangubai Kathiawadi

Release Date: Feb 25 2022

Director: Sanjay Leela Bhansali

Gangubai Kathiawadi (2022) is a biographical drama directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali, starring Alia Bhatt. The film follows Gangubai's journey from a young woman sold into prostitution to a powerful figure in Mumbai's Kamathipura district, where she advocates for the rights of women. It highlights themes of resilience and empowerment. The film showcases Alia Bhatt's powerful performance and Bhansali's signature visual style, filled with vibrant set designs, costumes, and intense storytelling.
