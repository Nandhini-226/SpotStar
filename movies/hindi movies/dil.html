Dil Bechara

Release Date: 24 July 2020

Director: Gauri Shinde

Based on John Green's novel The Fault in Our Stars, the film tells the story of Kizie Basu (Sanjana Sanghi), a young girl battling cancer, and Manny (Sushant Singh Rajput), a lively boy with his own health challenges. Their friendship grows into a deep bond as they embark on an unforgettable journey together, discovering love, hope, and the fragility of life. Dil Bechara holds special significance as it was Sushant Singh Rajput's last film before his untimely death, and it received an emotional response from audiences.
