Student of the Year

Release Date: 19 October 2012

Director: Karan Johar

The film revolves around the lives of three students, Rohan (Varun Dhawan), Abhimanyu (Sidharth Malhotra), and Shanaya (Alia Bhatt), who are competing for the title of "Student of the Year" at St. Teresa's School. The competition is intense, as it tests their academic, athletic, and personal capabilities, leading to friendship, rivalry, love, and betrayal. The film is known for its high-energy dance sequences, glamorous setting, and youthful energy. While it received mixed reviews for its plot, it was a commercial success and launched the careers of its lead actors. The movie's soundtrack, including songs like "Radha" and "Disco Deewane," became popular, making it a notable film in Bollywood's coming-of-age genre.
