Joker

Release Date: 2 October 2019

Director: Todd Phillips

Joker (2019) tells the story of Arthur Fleck, a mentally troubled man living in Gotham City, struggling to find his place in a harsh society. Working as a party clown and aspiring stand-up comedian, Arthur is repeatedly bullied and neglected. His descent into madness begins after a series of personal tragedies and violent outbursts. As he adopts the persona of the Joker, he sparks chaos and rebellion in the city, ultimately transforming into Gotham's infamous criminal mastermind and symbol of anarchy.
