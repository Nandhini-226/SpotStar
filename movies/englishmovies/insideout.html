Inside Out

Release Date: June 19, 2015

Director: Pete Docter

Inside Out follows 11-year-old Riley as she moves to a new city and struggles with her emotions. Inside her mind, five core emotions—Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust—work together to guide her. When Joy and Sadness are accidentally swept away from headquarters, Riley’s emotions fall into chaos, leaving only Anger, Fear, and Disgust in control. As Joy and Sadness journey back, they learn that all emotions are important in helping Riley navigate her life and find balance again.
