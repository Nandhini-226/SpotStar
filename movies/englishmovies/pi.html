Life of Pi

Release Date: 23 November 2012

Director: Ang Lee

Life of Pi tells the story of Pi Patel, a young boy from India, who survives a shipwreck and is stranded in the Pacific Ocean on a lifeboat with a Bengal tiger named Richard Parker. As Pi struggles to survive, he forms a unique bond with the tiger, relying on faith, ingenuity, and willpower to endure the hardships. The story explores themes of survival, spirituality, and the power of storytelling, leaving the audience to question what is real and what is imagined.
