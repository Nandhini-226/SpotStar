Retro Cinema

Jurassic Park

Release Date: 15 April 1994

Director: Steven Spielberg

The story centers on a revolutionary theme park where dinosaurs have been brought back to life through genetic engineering. When a group of experts, including paleontologists Dr. Alan Grant (Sam Neill) and Dr. Ellie Sattler (Laura Dern), and mathematician Dr. Ian Malcolm (Jeff Goldblum), are invited to preview the park, things take a disastrous turn when the dinosaurs escape their enclosures. The film is celebrated for its groundbreaking visual effects, thrilling narrative, and iconic moments, such as the T. rex attack and the chilling velociraptor scenes. Jurassic Park became a cultural phenomenon, revolutionized filmmaking with its use of CGI, and spawned a successful franchise.
