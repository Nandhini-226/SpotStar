Charlie And The Chocolate Factory

Release Date: July 10, 2005

Director: Tim Burton

Charlie Bucket, a poor boy who wins a golden ticket to tour Willy Wonka’s magical chocolate factory. Along with four other children—Augustus Gloop, Veruca Salt, Violet Beauregarde, and Mike Teavee—Charlie explores the fantastical factory filled with incredible inventions. However, each of the other children falls victim to their flaws, leading to their humorous downfalls. Charlie, the only well-behaved child, is the last one remaining. Wonka reveals that the grand prize is the factory itself, offering it to Charlie as his heir. Charlie accepts, and he and his family move in.
