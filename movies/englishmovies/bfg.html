BFG

Release Date: July 29, 2016

Director: Steven Spielerg

The BFG follows Sophie, a young orphan, who is taken to Giant Country by the Big Friendly Giant (BFG). Unlike other giants who eat humans, the BFG is kind and collects dreams for children. Sophie and the BFG team up to stop the other, more dangerous giants, led by Fleshlumpeater, who terrorize humans. With the help of the Queen of England, they devise a plan to capture the giants and save the human world. Throughout, a strong friendship forms between Sophie and the BFG.
