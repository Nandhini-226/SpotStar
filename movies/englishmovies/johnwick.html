John Wick

Release Date: 14 November 2014

Director: Chad Stahelski

John Wick: Chapter 1 follows retired hitman John Wick, who is grieving the death of his wife. She leaves him a final gift—a puppy named Daisy—to help him cope. When a group of Russian gangsters, led by Iosef Tarasov, steal his car and kill his dog, John comes out of retirement, seeking revenge. As he hunts down those responsible, he faces numerous enemies from his past. The film is packed with intense action as John reclaims his legendary status as a feared assassin.
