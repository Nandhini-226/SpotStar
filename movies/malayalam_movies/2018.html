Retro Cinema

2018

Release Date: 5 May 2023

Director: Jude Anthany Joseph

The film is based on the true events of the 2018 Kerala floods, one of the worst natural disasters to hit the state. It follows various characters from different walks of life who come together to survive and help each other during the catastrophe, showcasing the resilience and unity of the people of Kerala. The movie was widely praised for its realistic portrayal, gripping narrative, and powerful performances, particularly from Tovino Thomas. 2018 received acclaim for its emotional depth, intense cinematography, and visual effects that recreated the floods. It became a huge box-office success and was celebrated for its inspiring message of hope, humanity, and collective strength in the face of disaster.
