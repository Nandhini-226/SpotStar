Charlie

Release Date: 24 December 2015

Director: Martin Prakkat

The story follows Tessa (Parvathy), a free-spirited young woman who stumbles upon clues to the life of a mysterious man named Charlie (Dulquer Salmaan). Intrigued by his unconventional lifestyle and adventurous spirit, Tessa embarks on a journey to find him, discovering herself along the way. The film was celebrated for its unique storytelling, vibrant visuals, and memorable performances. Dulquer’s portrayal of Charlie, with his bohemian charm, and Parvathy’s nuanced performance as Tessa were widely praised.
