Super Sharanya

Release Date: 7 January 2022

Director: Girish A.D.

The film follows Sharanya (Anaswara Rajan), a young girl from a small town who moves to a big city for college. Shy and reserved, Sharanya finds herself navigating the challenges of college life, friendships, and romantic interests, leading to humorous and heartwarming moments as she learns to find her own voice. Super Sharanya was appreciated for its relatable storytelling, light-hearted humor, and strong performances, especially by Anaswara Rajan, who portrayed the awkwardness and growth of her character with charm. The film’s portrayal of college friendships, crushes, and the journey of self-discovery resonated with younger audiences, making it a fun and refreshing addition to Malayalam cinema’s coming-of-age genre.
