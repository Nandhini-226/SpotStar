King of Kotha

Release Date: 24 August 2023

Director: Abhilash Joshiy

The film is set in a fictional town called Kotha, controlled by crime and violence. Dulquer plays Raju, a former gangster who is forced to return to his criminal roots to reclaim control over the town and face old rivals. Known for its intense action sequences and stylish narration, King of Kotha explores themes of power, revenge, and redemption. Dulquer Salmaan’s performance and the film’s visual style were highlights, making it a significant entry in Malayalam cinema’s action genre.
