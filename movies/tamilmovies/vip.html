Velaiyilla Pattathari

Release Date:18 July 2014

Director: R. Velraj

The film follows Raghuvaran (Dhanush), an unemployed engineering graduate who faces constant criticism from his family and society due to his joblessness. Despite the challenges, he remains determined to secure a position in his field. When a tragedy strikes his family, Raghuvaran's life takes a significant turn, and he finally gets a chance to prove his skills and dedication as an engineer. The movie explores themes of ambition, resilience, and the struggles faced by unemployed youth. VIP was highly appreciated for Dhanush's performance, its relatable storyline, and Anirudh Ravichander's music, with hit songs like "Amma Amma" and "What a Karuvad."
