Kanaa

Release Date: 21 December 2018

Director: Arunraja Kamaraj

The film follows the story of Kousalya (Aishwarya Rajesh), a young woman from a rural village who dreams of becoming a cricketer. Despite facing various challenges, including gender bias and societal expectations, she receives support from her father, played by Sathyaraj, and strives to achieve her goal of representing the Indian women's cricket team. Kanaa is an inspiring tale of determination, perseverance, and breaking societal barriers. The film received critical acclaim for its strong performances, especially Aishwarya Rajesh's portrayal of the lead character, and its motivational storyline.
