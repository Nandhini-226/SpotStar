Good Night

Release Date: 12 May 2023

Director: Vinayak Chandrasekaran

The film revolves around a man named Raghavan (Manikandan), who struggles with a fear of sleeping due to childhood trauma. His unusual sleep disorder and the quirky events around it lead to a humorous and heartfelt journey of self-discovery. The movie combines elements of comedy, drama, and emotional depth, exploring the protagonist's relationship with his fears and the people around him. The film was appreciated for its fresh concept and engaging performances, making it an entertaining watch for fans of lighthearted, feel-good stories.
