Pritam Chakraborty

Music Composer, Playback Singer, Producer

About

Birthdate: June 14, 1971

Hometown: Kolkata, West Bengal, India

Pritam Chakraborty, widely known as Pritam, is one of the most prolific and successful music composers in Bollywood. Known for his versatility and memorable compositions, Pritam has created some of the most iconic soundtracks in Indian cinema.

Career Highlights

Bollywood Debut: Started his career with the film Tere Liye (2001). Rose to fame with Dhoom (2004), featuring blockbuster tracks like Dhoom Machale.

Notable Soundtracks: Known for chart-topping albums in films like Jab We Met, Ae Dil Hai Mushkil, Barfi!, Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani, Bajrangi Bhaijaan, and Brahmastra. Tracks like Channa Mereya, Tum Hi Ho Bandhu, and Bulleya have become cultural touchstones.

Achievements

Multiple Filmfare Awards and IIFA Awards for Best Music Direction.

Known for blending contemporary and traditional sounds in his compositions.

Worked with top singers like Arijit Singh, Shreya Ghoshal, and Sunidhi Chauhan.
