Sachet Tandon

Singer, Music Composer

About

Birthdate: August 17, 1989

Hometown:Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, India

Sachet Tandon is a celebrated Indian singer and composer, best known for his work as part of the music duo Sachet-Parampara. His melodious voice and impactful compositions have earned him fame in Bollywood and independent music.

Career Highlights

Rise to Fame: Gained popularity as a finalist in the reality show The Voice India (2015).

Bollywood Success: Known for chart-topping songs like Bekhayali (Kabir Singh), which became a cultural phenomenon. Other hits include Mere Sohneya, Maiyya Mainu, and Raatan Lambiyan (Shershaah).

Music Composition: As part of Sachet-Parampara, has composed memorable soundtracks for movies like Toilet: Ek Prem Katha, Kabir Singh, Shershaah, and Jersey.

Awards

Winner of several prestigious awards, including Filmfare and IIFA Awards, for his contributions to Bollywood music.

Nominated for his acting performances in critically acclaimed films.
