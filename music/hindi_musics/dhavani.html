Dhvani Bhanushali

Playback Singer, Pop Artist

About

Birthdate: March 22, 1998

Hometown: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Dhvani Bhanushali is a rising star in the Indian music industry, known for her melodious voice and chart-topping singles. She gained fame through her Bollywood songs and independent pop hits, earning her the title of India’s “Pop Princess.”

Career Highlights

Bollywood Debut: Made her playback debut with the song Ishtehaar from the movie Welcome to New York (2018). Gained recognition with hits like Dilbar (Satyameva Jayate) and Psycho Saiyaan (Saaho).

Independent Hits: Her single Vaaste became a viral sensation, amassing over a billion views on YouTube and earning her widespread acclaim.

Achievements

Youngest Indian singer to reach the 1-billion mark on YouTube for a single song (Vaaste).

Multiple awards and nominations for her playback and independent music contributions.

Known for her ability to perform across genres, from romantic ballads to dance numbers.
