Muhammad Atif Aslam

Playback Singer, Actor

About

Birthdate: March 12, 1983

Hometown: Wazirabad, Punjab, Pakistan

Atif Aslam is a celebrated Pakistani playback singer and actor known for his soulful and powerful voice. His music transcends borders, making him a beloved figure in both Bollywood and the Pakistani music industry.

Career Highlights

Early Success: Began his career with the band Jal, releasing the iconic song Aadat (2004), which gained immense popularity across South Asia.

Bollywood Career: Debuted in Bollywood with Woh Lamhe from Zeher (2005). Delivered numerous hit songs like Tera Hone Laga Hoon, Jeene Laga Hoon, Tajdar-e-Haram, Dil Diyan Gallan, and Pehli Nazar Mein.

Achievements

Winner of multiple awards, including Lux Style Awards and Filmfare nominations.

Known for his live performances and international tours, showcasing his vocal prowess.

Made his acting debut with the Pakistani film Bol (2011), which received critical acclaim.
