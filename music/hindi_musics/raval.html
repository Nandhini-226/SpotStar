Darshan Raval

Singer, Composer, Lyricist

About

Birthdate: October 18, 1994

Hometown: Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India

Darshan Raval is a versatile and popular Indian singer known for his heartwarming voice and relatable songs. He has made a significant mark in independent music and Bollywood playback singing, captivating audiences with his melodies..

Career Highlights

Rise to Fame: Gained recognition as a finalist in the reality show India's Raw Star (2014). Released his breakout single Tera Zikr, which became a massive hit.

Bollywood Hits: Sung chart-topping tracks like Chogada (Loveyatri), Kamariya (Mitron), and Ek Ladki Ko Dekha Toh Aisa Laga (title track).

Independent Music: Known for singles like Hawa Banke, Bhula Dunga, and Asal Mein.

Achievements

Winner of several awards for his independent music and Bollywood songs.

Recognized for blending modern sounds with traditional melodies.
