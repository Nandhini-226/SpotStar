Armaan Malik

Singer, Songwriter, Composer

About

Birthdate: July 22, 1995

Hometown: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Armaan Malik is a versatile playback singer and composer known for his melodious voice and wide range of musical styles. He has sung in multiple languages, making him a favorite in both Bollywood and independent music scenes.

Career Highlights

Musical Roots: Son of music director Daboo Malik and nephew of renowned composer Anu Malik. Trained in classical music and began his career at a young age.

Bollywood Success: Rose to fame with songs like Main Hoon Hero Tera (Hero), Bol Do Na Zara (Azhar), and Jab Tak (M.S. Dhoni).

Independent Music: Ventured into independent music with tracks like Control and You. Known for his international collaborations, making him a global pop icon.

Achievements

Winner of multiple awards, including Filmfare and Global Indian Music Academy (GiMA) awards.

Recognized for his multilingual singing in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali, and English.
