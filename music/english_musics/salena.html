Selena Marie Gomez

Singer, Actress, Producer, Businesswoman

About

Birthdate: July 22, 1992

Hometown: Grand Prairie, Texas, USA

Selena Gomez is an American singer, actress, and producer who gained fame both for her successful music career and her acting roles. Known for her versatile talents, she has built a global fanbase through her music, acting, and philanthropic work.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Gained fame as the star of Disney Channel's Wizards of Waverly Place (2007-2012).

Music Career: Lead singer of the band Selena Gomez & the Scene, releasing hits like Love You Like a Love Song. Launched her solo career with albums like Stars Dance (2013) and Rare (2020), featuring hits like Come & Get It and Lose You to Love Me.

Achievements

Over 25 million albums sold worldwide.

Multiple award wins, including Billboard Women in Music awards and American Music Awards.

Executive producer of the hit series 13 Reasons Why (2017-2020) and Only Murders in the Building (2021).
