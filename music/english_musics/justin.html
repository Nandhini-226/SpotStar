Justin Drew Bieber

Singer, Songwriter, Actor

About

Birthdate: March 1, 1994

Hometown: London, Ontario, Canada

Justin Bieber is a global pop icon who rose to fame as a teen sensation and evolved into one of the most successful and versatile artists of his generation. Known for his captivating voice and diverse music styles, he remains a cultural phenomenon in the music industry.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Rose to fame with My World (2009), featuring the global hit Baby. Chart-Topping

Albums: Delivered smash hits with Believe (2012), Purpose (2015), and Justice (2021).

Cultural Icon: Known for evolving music styles and a massive global fanbase.

Achievements

Over 150 million records sold worldwide.

2 Grammy Awards and numerous Billboard Music Awards..

Youngest solo male artist to have 8 No. 1 albums on the Billboard 200.
