Shawn Peter Raul Mendes

Singer, Songwriter

About

Birthdate: August 8, 1998

Hometown: Pickering, Ontario, Canada

Shawn Mendes is a Canadian singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and acoustic guitar-driven pop songs. Rising to fame with his viral Vine videos, he quickly became a global pop sensation with his heartfelt music and relatable lyrics.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Gained popularity with his Vine covers in 2013, leading to his debut single Life of the Party (2014).

Chart-Topping Albums: Handwritten (2015): Included the hit single Stitches.Illuminate (2016) and Shawn Mendes (2018) produced hits like Treat You Better and There's Nothing Holdin' Me Back.

Achievements

Over 70 million records sold worldwide..

Multiple award wins, including American Music Awards and MTV Europe Music Awards.
