Dua Lipa

Singer, Songwriter, Model

About

Birthdate: August 22, 19957

Hometown: London, England

Dua Lipa is a British singer and songwriter who became one of the biggest pop stars of her generation with her powerful voice, catchy hits, and bold fashion sense. Known for her deep, sultry vocals and innovative music, she quickly rose to fame and established herself as a pop music icon.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Debut self-titled album Dua Lipa (2017) featured global hits like New Rules, earning her critical acclaim and commercial success.

Chart-Topping Success: Future Nostalgia (2020): A game-changing album that included massive hits like Don't Start Now and Physical, solidifying her pop dominance.

Achievements

Won Grammy Awards for Best New Artist (2019) and Best Pop Vocal Album (2021).

Known for her stylish music videos and boundary-pushing collaborations.
