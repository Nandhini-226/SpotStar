Harry Edward Styles

Singer, Songwriter, Actor

About

Birthdate: February 1, 1994

Hometown: Redditch, Worcestershire, England

Harry Styles is an English singer-songwriter and actor, best known as a member of the global boy band One Direction. Following the band's hiatus, he launched a successful solo career, blending pop, rock, and folk influences with his distinctive voice and bold style.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Rose to fame as a member of One Direction after they finished third on The X Factor (2010). One Direction became one of the best-selling music artists in history with hits like What Makes You Beautiful and Story of My Life.

Solo Career: Released his debut solo album Harry Styles (2017), featuring hits like Sign of the Times and Kiwi.

Achievements

Over 70 million records sold worldwide.

Multiple award wins, including Brit Awards and Grammy nominations.

Known for his eclectic fashion, breaking boundaries in gender expression and redefining pop-star masculinity.
