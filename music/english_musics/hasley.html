Ashley Nicolette Frangipane

Singer, Songwriter

About

Birthdate: September 29, 1994

Hometown: Edison, New Jersey, USA

Halsey is an American singer and songwriter known for her genre-blending music that combines elements of pop, electropop, indie, and alternative. Her deeply personal lyrics and bold, unique style have made her a standout artist in the music industry.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Gained attention with her debut EP Room 93 (2014) and breakout single Ghost in 2015. Released Badlands (2015), featuring hits like New Americana and Colors, which brought her mainstream success.

Chart-Topping Success: Hopeless Fountain Kingdom (2017): Produced hits like Now or Never and Bad at Love. Manic (2020): Included chart-toppers such as Without Me and You Should Be Sad, solidifying her status as a pop powerhouse.

Achievements/h1>

Over 50 million albums sold worldwide.

Multiple award nominations, including Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and MTV Video Music Awards.
