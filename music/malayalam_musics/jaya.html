M. Jayachandran

Music Composer, Playback Singer

About

Birthdate: June 14, 1971

Hometown: Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, India

M. Jayachandran is a celebrated Indian music composer and singer who has made significant contributions to Malayalam cinema. Known for his soulful melodies, he has been a dominant figure in the industry for decades.

Career Highlights

Extensive Work: Composed music for over 100 Malayalam films.

Hit Songs: Known for evergreen tracks like Aaromale (Millennium Stars), Sundari Sundari (Achanurangatha Veedu), and Kannodu Chollanu (Ravanaprabhu).Versatility: Excels in creating classical, semi-classical, and contemporary music styles.

Awards

Kerala State Film Awards: Won Best Music Director 7 times.

National Film Award: Best Music Direction for the film Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015).

Filmfare Awards South: Multiple wins for his remarkable compositions.
