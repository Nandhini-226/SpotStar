Rajesh Murugesan

Music Composer

About

Birthdate: May 14, 1988

Hometown: Kerala, India

Rajesh Murugesan is a renowned Indian music composer primarily associated with Malayalam cinema. Known for his innovative and youthful soundtracks, he has gained significant recognition for redefining film music with his unique style.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Gained widespread fame with his debut soundtrack for the film Neram (2013), directed by Alphonse Puthren.

Iconic Soundtrack: Achieved massive success with the music for Premam (2015), delivering chart-topping songs like Malare and Aluva Puzha.

Collaboration: Frequently collaborates with Alphonse Puthren, creating memorable and fresh music for his films.

Awards

Asianet Film Awards: Best Music Director for Premam.

Vanitha Film Awards: Recognized for his outstanding contribution to the film Premam.

South Indian International Movie Awards (SIIMA): Honored for his work in Malayalam cinema.
