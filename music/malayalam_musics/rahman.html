Shaan Rahman

Music Composer, Singer

About

Birthdate: December 30, 1979

Hometown: Thalassery, Kerala, India

Shaan Rahman is a prominent Indian music composer and singer who primarily works in Malayalam cinema. Known for his melodious and youthful compositions, he has created several iconic tracks that have captured the hearts of audiences across India.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Achieved fame with the soundtrack for Thattathin Marayathu (2012), which became a milestone in Malayalam cinema music.

Viral Hits: Created iconic tracks like Jimikki Kammal (Velipadinte Pusthakam) and Malare (Premam), which gained international popularity.

Collaborations: Frequently works with director Vineeth Sreenivasan, delivering memorable soundtracks for his films.

Awards

South Indian International Movie Awards (SIIMA): Recognized for his exceptional compositions in Malayalam cinema.

Asianet Film Awards: Honored for his contributions to hit soundtracks like Premam.
