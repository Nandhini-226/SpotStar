Sushin Shyam

Music Composer, Singer

About

Birthdate: February 13, 1992

Hometown: Thrissur, Kerala, India

Sushin Shyam is an accomplished Indian music composer and singer known for his groundbreaking work in Malayalam cinema. With his expertise in blending traditional and contemporary sounds, Sushin has carved a niche as one of the most innovative composers in the industry.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough: Rose to prominence with his background scores for critically acclaimed films like Kumbalangi Nights (2019) and Eeda (2018).

Iconic Soundtracks: Composed memorable songs for films such as Varathan (2018), Virus (2019), and Malik (2021).

Collaborations: Frequently works with leading directors in Malayalam cinema, showcasing his ability to adapt to diverse storytelling styles.

Awards

Kerala State Film Award: Best Background Score for Kumbalangi Nights.

Filmfare Awards South: Best Music Director nomination for his innovative scores.

South Indian International Movie Awards (SIIMA): Honored for his contributions to Malayalam cinema.
