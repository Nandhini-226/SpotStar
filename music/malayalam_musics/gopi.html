Gopi Sundar

Music Composer, Singer

About

Birthdate: May 30, 1977

Hometown: Kochi, Kerala, India

Gopi Sundar is a celebrated Indian composer and playback singer renowned for his versatile contributions to South Indian cinema. With a career spanning over two decades, he has become a defining musical voice in Malayalam, Telugu, and Tamil films, blending tradition with contemporary styles.

Career Highlights

breakthrough: Gopi Sundar made his entry as a composer with the Malayalam film Kaiyoppu in 2006, marking the beginning of his prolific career in South Indian cinema.

Notable Films: His music in films like Bangalore Days, Charlie, Premam, and Pulimurugan have received critical acclaim and commercial success.

Awards

National Film Award for Best Background Score for 1983

Kerala State Film Award, and multiple Filmfare Awards South.
