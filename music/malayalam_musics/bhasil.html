Sreenath Bhasi

Actor, Singer

About

Birthdate:May 29, 1988

Hometown: Kochi, Kerala, India

Sreenath Bhasi is a versatile Indian actor and singer primarily associated with the Malayalam film industry. Known for his natural acting style and soulful singing, he has gained a reputation as a talented artist capable of portraying diverse roles and delivering memorable performances.

Career Highlights

Acting Career: Made his acting debut with Pranayam (2011) and gained fame with roles in films like Da Thadiya, Ustad Hotel, and Jacobinte Swargarajyam.

Playback Singing: Contributed his vocal talent to songs in films like North 24 Kaatham and Honey Bee.

Versatility: Known for balancing roles across mainstream films and experimental cinema.

Awards

Best Actor (Special Mention) for his role in Kumbalangi Nights (2019).Kerala State Film Award

SIIMA Awards Nominated for Best Actor for his performances in various films, including Home (2021).

Film Critics' Awards Recognized for his contributions to Malayalam cinema with multiple nominations in the Best Actor category for his diverse roles.
