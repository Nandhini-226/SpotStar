Anirudh Ravichander

Indian Music Composer, Singer, and Producer

About

Birthdate: October 16, 1990

Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

He is an Indian film composer and singer. He made his debut in the Tamil film Name 3,His subsequent albums have all become commercial successes and he has won acclaim from critics for his soundtrack music and background recordings.

Carrer Highlights

Breakthrough: Gained international fame with the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di" from 3 (2012).

Film Music: Composed chart-topping tracks for Tamil blockbusters such as Vedalam, Kaththi, Master, and Jailer.

Singing: Known for his versatile singing in hit tracks like "Selfie Pulla," "Chellamma," and "Arabic Kuthu."

Awards

Numerous Filmfare Awards for Best Music Director.

Multiple SIIMA Awards and Vijay Awards for his outstanding compositions.

Recognized as one of the youngest composers to achieve pan-Indian fame
