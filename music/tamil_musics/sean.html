Raghavendra Raja Rao (Sean Roldan)

Indian Music Composer, Singer, and Lyricist

About

Birthdate: March 11, 1987

Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

he is a multi-talented music composer and playback singer known for his versatility in Tamil cinema. With a background in Carnatic music and a flair for experimentation,His work in both indie and film music has earned him critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

Career Highlights

Debut as Composer: Made his debut as a music director in Tamil cinema with Vaanavil (2014), though he gained major recognition with his work in Muthina Kathirika (2016).

Notable Compositions: Gained praise for his work in films like Kaaviya Thalaivan, Ratsasan, Kaatru Veliyidai, and Aruvi, known for their soulful and innovative scores.

Achievements

Nominated for multiple Filmfare Awards South.

Praised for his ability to handle diverse genres and themes.
