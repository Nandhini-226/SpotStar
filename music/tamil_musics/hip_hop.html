Adhithya Ramachandran Venkatapathy (Hiphop Tamizha Aadhi)

Music Composer, Rapper, Lyricist, Actor, Director

About

Birthdate: February 20, 1990

Hometown: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India

Hiphop Aadhi, is a trailblazer in the Tamil music and independent rap scene. He is credited with introducing Tamil hip-hop to a broader audience and is known for his versatile talent as a composer, lyricist, singer, and actor. With chart-topping albums and successful ventures in acting and direction.

Career Highlights

Independent Music: Gained recognition with the independent album Club Le Mabbu Le, which became a viral hit. Entered Tamil cinema as a music composer with the film Aambala (2015).

Successful Compositions: hit movies like Thani Oruvan, Meesaya Murukku, Natpe Thunai

Acting and Directing: Made his acting and directorial debut with Meesaya Murukku which was both a critical and commercial success.

Awards

Edison Award: Best Debut Music Director for Aambala (2015).

SIIMA Nomination: Best Music Director for Thani Oruvan (2015).

Cultural Impact: Recognized for popularizing Tamil hip-hop globally.
