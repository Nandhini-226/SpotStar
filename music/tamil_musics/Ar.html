AR RAHMAN - Indian Music Composer

Indian Music Composer, Singer, and Producer

A.R. Rahman

Birthdate: January 6, 1967

Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

A.R. Rahman, famously known as the "Mozart of Madras",is an internationally acclaimed Indian composer, music director, and record producer. Renowned for his unique blend of traditional Indian music and modern orchestral arrangements, Rahman has revolutionized the Indian music industry.

Career Highlights

Indian Cinema: Achieved fame with Roja (1992) and delivered iconic soundtracks for films like Bombay, Dil Se, and Lagaan.

Global Reach: Won two Academy Awards for Slumdog Millionaire and worked on international projects like 127 Hours and Bombay Dreams.

Collaborations: Partnered with global artists such as Mick Jagger and Michael Jackson, elevating Indian music globally.
