Pradeep Kumar

Indian Playback Singer, Music Composer, and Lyricist

About

Birthdate: May 12, 1986

Hometown: Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India

Pradeep Kumar is a celebrated playback singer and music composer in the Tamil film industry, known for his soulful voice and minimalistic approach to music. His songs often evoke deep emotions, making him a favorite for melodies and introspective tracks.

Career Highlights

Breakthrough Song: Gained significant recognition with songs like "Enna Solla Pogirai" from Kandukondain Kandukondain (2000) and "Pookal Pookum" from Vaaranam Aayiram (2008).

Popular Soundtracks: Contributed to hit tracks in films like Sundarapandian, Vikram Vedha, Kumki, and Raavanan.

Achievements

Won Filmfare and Vijay Awards for his playback singing.

Known for contributions to critically acclaimed films.
