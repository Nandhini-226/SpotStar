Harris Jayaraj

Indian Music Composer and Playback Singer

About

Birthdate: January 8, 1975

Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

Harris Jayaraj is one of the most successful and celebrated music composers in Indian cinema, especially known for his contributions to Tamil films. His music is characterized by its melodious tunes, innovative orchestration, and modern arrangements.

Career Highlights

Debut Success: Made an impactful debut with Minnale (2001), earning critical acclaim and numerous awards, including a Filmfare Award for Best Music Director.

Hitmaker: Known for crafting evergreen soundtracks for films like Kaakha Kaakha, Vettaiyaadu Vilaiyaadu, Ghajini, Ayan, and Ko.

Collaboration: Frequently collaborates with leading filmmakers like Gautham Vasudev Menon and K.V. Anand.

Awards

Multiple Filmfare Awards and Vijay Awards for Best Music Direction.

Recognized as one of the most consistent hitmakers in Tamil cinema.
