G.V. Prakash Kumar

Indian Music Composer, Playback Singer, and Actor

About

Birthdate: June 13, 1987

Hometown: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

G.V. Prakash Kumar is a renowned composer, singer, and actor in the Tamil film industry. Starting as a music composer, he gained fame for his soulful melodies and energetic tracks. He later transitioned into acting and has established himself as a versatile artist.

Career Highlights

Early Career: Started as a vocalist and composer, gaining attention with the song “Chikku Bukku Rayile” from Gentleman (1993), composed by A.R. Rahman.

Film Scoring Debut: Rose to fame with his debut as a music director in Veyyil (2006),Composed for blockbuster Tamil films such as Aadukalam, Darling, Theri, and Jail.

Acting Career: Successfully transitioned to acting with notable performances in films like Darling, Trisha Illana Nayanthara.

Achievements

Won multiple Filmfare Awards and Vijay Awards for his compositions.

Nominated for his acting performances in critically acclaimed films.
