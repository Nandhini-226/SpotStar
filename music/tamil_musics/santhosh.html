Santhosh Narayanan

Indian Music Composer, Background Scorer, and Playback Singer

Santhosh Narayanan

Birthdate: May 15, 1983

Hometown: Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India

He is a critically acclaimed music composer in Tamil cinema, known for his experimental and genre-defying soundtracks. His music is recognized for blending traditional Tamil folk elements with modern electronic, jazz, and rock influences. He is highly regarded for his impactful background scores.

Career Highlights

Debut as Composer: Gained recognition with his breakthrough score for Attakathi (2012), which earned him a place in the Tamil film music industry.

Versatility in Sound: Known for blending various genres, including folk, electronic, and contemporary music, creating a unique identity.

Notable Works: Composed music for critically acclaimed films like Pizza, Jigarthanda, Kabali, Vikram Vedha, and Super Deluxe.

Achievements

Filmfare and Vijay Awards for Best Music Director.

Recognized internationally for his work in Kabali and Kaala.

2 Grammy Awards

1 BAFTA Award

1 Golden Globe Award
